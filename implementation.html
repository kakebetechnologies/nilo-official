How We Deliver

NiSA operates through a collaborative implementation model that ensures efficiency, avoids duplication, and maximizes impact across the shea value chain.

1

Member-Led

Activities initiated and managed by NiSA members — cooperatives, private sector, and community groups driving local-level change.

2

Secretariat-Supported

Technical assistance, coordination, and resource mobilisation provided by the NiSA secretariat to strengthen member initiatives.

3

Secretariat-Led

Strategic activities directly managed by NiSA — policy advocacy, international representation, and sector-wide coordination.

Implementation Principles

Collaboration

Working together with members, partners, and stakeholders for shared outcomes.

Sustainability

Ensuring long-term environmental and economic viability in everything we do.

Inclusivity

Ensuring all actors, especially women and smallholders, have a voice and role.

Accountability

Transparent processes, measurable outcomes, and responsible resource use.

Building a Resilient Shea Sector

Our implementation model puts communities at the centre, ensuring every intervention is demand-driven and sustainable.

Get Involved

The Nilotica Shea Alliance (NiSA) is dedicated to building a sustainable, competitive, and inclusive shea industry in Uganda.
