Why Nilotica Shea?

Premium grade Vitellaria Nilotica from the Nile region — prized by formulators for its unique composition.

Softer Texture

Naturally soft and creamy at room temperature (melting point 26-32°C), requiring no excessive heating for formulation.

Higher Olein Content

Contains 50-65% oleic acid — significantly higher than West African varieties — for faster absorption and a silkier skin feel.

Cosmetic-Grade Quality

Exceptionally high in essential fatty acids, antioxidants, phytosterols, and Vitamin E (tocopherols) for premium formulations.

Elevate Your Cosmetic Portfolio

Skin Benefits
  • Ultra hydrating & fast absorbing
  • Rich in Vitamin E, phytosterols & triterpenes
  • Skin regeneration & anti-inflammatory (linoleic acid 4-9%)
  • Boosts collagen & repairs skin barrier
Key Applications in Cosmetic Formulations
  • Luxury skincare: Body butters, facial creams, anti-aging serums
  • Haircare: Deep conditioning masks, scalp treatments
  • Sun care & repair: After sun lotions, SPF formulations
  • Lip care & balms: Highly emollient for soft, nourished lips
