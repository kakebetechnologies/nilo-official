Who We Are

Uganda's Leading Shea Industry Body

The Nilotica Shea Alliance is the leading industry body for premium Nilotica (Vitellaria Nilotica) shea products from the Nile region of East Africa. We connect women-led co-operatives, producers, and export-ready SMEs with global cosmetic manufacturers and bulk ingredient buyers.

Our members produce shea butter that is naturally softer, higher in oleic acid, and superior in skin absorption compared to other shea varieties — making it the preferred choice for luxury skincare formulations worldwide.
