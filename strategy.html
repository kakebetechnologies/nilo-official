Our Strategic Framework

NiSA's five-year strategy is built on four pillars that transform Uganda's shea industry into a sustainable and inclusive sector.

Improved Quality of Shea & Shea-Based Products

We elevate Nilotica shea quality through standardised grading, targeted training, and processing innovation to meet international benchmarks.

  • Quality grading standards
  • Collector & processor training
  • Testing & certification
  • Value-added development

Improved Market Access

We connect shea producers with local and global buyers, positioning Nilotica shea as a premium ingredient across cosmetic, food, and pharmaceutical markets.

  • Trade linkage facilitation
  • Export readiness support
  • Market information systems
  • Global market promotion

Improved Climate Smart Practices

We champion sustainable harvesting, shea tree conservation, and climate-resilient practices to protect parklands and sustain communities.

  • Sustainable harvesting
  • Tree planting & conservation
  • Community climate resilience
  • Green value chain integration

Conducive Enabling Environment

We advocate for supportive policies, strong institutions, and strategic partnerships to help the shea value chain flourish.

  • Policy advocacy
  • Institutional coordination
  • Strategic partnerships
  • Research & development

The Nilotica Shea Alliance (NiSA) is dedicated to building a sustainable, competitive, and inclusive shea industry in Uganda.
